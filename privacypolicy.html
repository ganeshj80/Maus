Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 December 2025

1. Overview

The mausprivacypolicy SPFx web part (“the App”) runs entirely within Microsoft SharePoint. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles data when installed or used.

2. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data outside of your Microsoft 365 tenant. It only accesses SharePoint data that the current user is authorized to access, such as:

  • SharePoint lists and libraries
  • Site or page context information
  • User profile information (if explicitly required by the App)

3. Data Usage

All data accessed by the App is used solely to provide its intended functionality within SharePoint. The App does not:

  • Log user activity
  • Track users
  • Use cookies or local storage for tracking
  • Perform analytics outside Microsoft 365

4. Data Storage

The App does not store any data externally. All data remains within your Microsoft 365 tenant and is subject to Microsoft’s security and compliance policies.

5. Data Sharing

The App does not share data with third parties or transmit data externally, except as explicitly documented in the App description.

6. Permissions

The App only requests the minimum SharePoint and Microsoft Graph permissions required to function. Permissions are clearly declared in the solution manifest and require tenant admin approval.

7. Security

The App follows Microsoft SPFx development best practices and relies on SharePoint and Microsoft 365 security features to protect data.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not designed for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
